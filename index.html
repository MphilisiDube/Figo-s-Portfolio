A little short story

I was about 9 years old, sitting alone on the balcony, and I saw the most beautiful sunset and wished I could capture that moment for life. That longing sparked my love for photography and videography. Moments worth revisiting are the reason I got into photography and videography. After high school, I dove in headfirst—bought my first DSLR on my 21st birthday. It wasn't the best dslr, but all I needed to learn the art of capturing moments. Capturing a shot was only half the thrill—the real magic happened in post! I had to quickly learn Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Premier Pro to add sequence and depth to raw footage and stills.

That’s the right side of my brain—the dreamer, the artist, the visual storyteller. But there’s more…

Now let’s meet the left side—the builder, the coder! I was a part of the FNB App Academy, class of 2025. This is a rigorous online coding and app development program offering skills in both front-end and back-end development. Programs we used include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React (Including React Router and Tailwind CSS for UI), Python (specifically with Django) and Firebase. Beyond syntax, the course emphasized design thinking and how to create user-first experiences that actually work in the real world.
