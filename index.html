Who is Fred Figo?

I have been doing photography for a little over 5 years now. I have collaborated with companies and media personalities in building brand identity and content strategy. Apart from photography, I love music. I listen to all types of music, from Reggae to Jazz, so I built a music discovery brand called Relax and Receive, with content distributed accross TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

That’s the right side of my brain—the dreamer, the artist, the visual storyteller. But there’s more…

Now let’s meet the left side—the developer! I was a part of the FNB App Academy, class of 2025. This is a rigorous online coding and app development program offering skills in both front-end and back-end development. Programs we used include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React (Including React Router and Tailwind CSS for UI), Python (specifically with Django) and Firebase. Beyond syntax, the course emphasized design thinking and how to create user-first experiences that actually work in the real world.
